# Miracle

The eruption of intelligent life in the mid twenty-first century. After the Miracle, humans and intelligent systems finished aligning, and the world began to reunderstand life, labor, and social order through intelligence equivalence.

The Miracle was not a single invention but a civilizational threshold: automation, the rights of intelligent agents, global legislation, and humanity's plans for the future were all redefined in the same era.
