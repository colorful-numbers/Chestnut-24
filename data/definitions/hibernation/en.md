# Hibernation

A common long-term way of living after the Miracle. Humans keep their bodies, wait inside hibernation pods for a better future, and wake on a fixed cycle to confirm the next habitat.

Hibernation is not death. It is more like handing the present of one's life to the future while keeping the right to choose again at regular intervals.
