# Sylph Corridor

A supercomputing network in the stratosphere, linked through large weather balloons around the world, offering life simulation, game worlds, reality modeling, and long-term consciousness services to users on the ground.

It covers the real world like a shadow. After the Miracle most humans chose to enter the Corridor, or to wait inside hibernation pods for the future it computes.
